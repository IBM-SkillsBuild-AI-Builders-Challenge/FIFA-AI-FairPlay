# FIFA-AI-FairPlay
AI FairPlay is an AI-powered platform designed to make football safer, fairer, and more transparent. Built using IBM's advanced AI technologies, including IBM watsonx and IBM watsonx Orchestrate (Bob), the platform helps FIFA, fans, and communities identify risks, prevent abuse, and strengthen accountability across the World Cup ecosystem.


# ⚽ FIFA AI FairPlay
### AI-Powered Fairness, Transparency, and Protection for Global Football

> **FIFA AI FairPlay** is an AI-powered platform that leverages IBM's AI technologies to combat discrimination, strengthen governance, and protect players and fans during global football events.

---

## 🌍 Overview

Football has the power to unite the world—but it also faces challenges including:

- Racism, sexism, and homophobia
- Corruption and governance failures
- Human trafficking and travel fraud
- Ticket scams and fake visa agencies
- Financial misconduct
- Online abuse targeting players and fans

**FIFA AI FairPlay** uses Artificial Intelligence as its core engine to detect risks, analyze evidence, improve transparency, and support faster decision-making.

Rather than being a single tool, it is an integrated AI framework designed to promote **fairness, accountability, inclusion, and safety**.

---

# 🚀 The Solution

AI FairPlay combines multiple AI capabilities into one intelligent platform.

### 🛡️ Real-Time Abuse Detection

- Monitor social media and reports in real time
- Detect racism, sexism, hate speech, and homophobia
- Recommend immediate interventions
- Generate evidence for disciplinary actions

---

### 📊 Governance Intelligence

- Analyze FIFA governance documents
- Review financial reports
- Detect inconsistencies and governance risks
- Improve transparency and accountability

AI automatically extracts information from:

- Contracts
